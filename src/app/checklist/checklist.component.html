Questionnaire

1. Client Details

  • Name: Full Name of the working client
  • Contact Address: Residence & Permanent addresses
  • Family Members: Number of dependents, etc.

2. Monthly Income Details

  • Net Income: Post deductions
  • Gross Income / CTC
  • Incentives & Bonus
  • Allowances (HRA, LTA, etc.)

3. Work Experience

  • Current Company: Name & Designation
  • Tenure: Years in current company
  • Previous History: Details if current is < 3 years

4. Assets & Residence

  • Assets: House, Car, Two-wheeler, etc.
  • Residence: Type (Rented/Owned), Locality, Tenure
  • Legality: DTCP/Plan Approval, Municipal limits

5. Property & Tax Details

  • Classification: Resi/Comm/Ind/Agri
  • Area: Land, Built-up & UDS
  • Stats: Age, Approval Authority, Property Tax

6. Other Income

  • Co-applicants: Income from other members
  • Rental: Amount, Tenants, Agreement details
  • Commissions & Other sources

7. Monthly Obligations & Liabilities

  • Credit Card & EMI
  • Car & Auto (Car/Bike) Loans
  • Personal Loans (PL)
  • Staff / Society Loans
  • Consumer Loans

  • Outside Borrowing details
  • Loan details: Amount, Tenure, Bank, ROI, EMI, Outstanding

Questionnaire

1. Client Details

  • Name: Full Name of the client
  • Contact Address: Residence, Business & Permanent addresses
  • Family Members: Number of dependents, etc.

2. Company Type

  • Partnership / Public Ltd / Private Ltd
  • Proprietorship / Self Employed / Others

3. Occupation Details

  • Industry/Sector: Kind of activity (Trading, Mfg, etc.)
  • Secondary Businesses: Other relevant details
  • Number of Branches: Reach of the business

4. Office Details

  • Type: Independent, Shop, Factory, Shared, etc.
  • Location: Industrial area, Shop, Plant, Residential

5. Business & Ownership

  • Duration: Current & Prior entities, Sister concerns
  • Ownership Status: Office, Current & Permanent residence
  • Office Building: Estimated area & value, Mandatory licenses

6. Assets & Residence

  • Assets: Properties, Car, Two-wheeler, etc.
  • Residence: Locality (Independent/Flat), Tenure, Legality (DTCP Approval)

7. Land Classification

  • Classification: Resi/Comm/Ind/Agri
  • Area Details: Land, Built-up & Undivided Share (UDS)
  • Property Stats: Age, Plan Approval Authority, Property Tax

8. Financial Management

  • Management: Number of partners & employees
  • Performance: Annual Turnover & Profit (Last 3 Years)
  • Expenses: CAPEX, OPEX, Working Capital, Inventory

9. Monthly Obligations & Liabilities

  • Credit Card & EMI
  • Car & Auto (Car/Bike) Loans
  • Personal Loans (PL)
  • Staff / Society Loans
  • Consumer Loans
  • Property Loans (Check for BT)
  • Outside Borrowing details
  • Loan details: Amount, Tenure, Bank, ROI, EMI, Outstanding

10. Revenue & Stock

  • Other Income: Rental, Commissions, Co-applicants
  • Rent Details: Tenants, Rent amount, Registered Agreement
  • Stock: Moving, Godown, Business & Dead stock

11. Operational Stats

  • Daily Cash Flow: Cash/Bank Payments & Receipts
  • Employee Strength: Permanent vs Temporary
  • Network: Major clients/suppliers & contribution to turnover
